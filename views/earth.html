Earth



Earth is the third closest planet to the Sun and the largest terrestrial planet.

It is the only planet not named after a god or goddess and the only planet known to support life.

It is the densest planet in the solar system despite only being the fifth largest in terms of size and mass. Earth has an average density of 5.52 g/c3.
